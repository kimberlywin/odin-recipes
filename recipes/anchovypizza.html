Boquerones & Lion's Mane Pizza

Description

Copycat of Flour & Water's marvelous 'Salsiccia Pizza' from 7/29/23.

Recipe from this website.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Caciocavallo or mozzarella cheese
  • Lion's mane mushroom
  • 6filet boquerones
  • 1tbsp olive oil
  • 100g pork sausage
  • 30g capers
  • 1/4 Sliced fresh chili
  • 60g Green split olive
  • Calabrian chilis to serve

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to the highest setting with a pizza stone inside.
  2. Roll dough out into around 30cm circles and add ingredients as above making sure spread them out evenly.
  3. Cook according to pizza dough instructions.
  4. Slice pizza into 6 pieces.
  5. Finish with 1 boquerone filet on top of each pizza slice.
