Bucatini Cacio e Pepe

Description

This is a recipe for delicious cacio e pepe with lion's mane mushrooms, an attempted copycat of the 'Black Garlic Bucatini' from Flour and Water on 7/29/23.

Recipe from this website.

Ingredients

  • 5oz bucatini
  • Black garlic
  • 1.5c freshly grate parmesan
  • 2tbsp olive oil
  • 2tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1c smoked lion's mane mushrooms
  • Salt & freshly cracked black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Cook pasta according to the package. Drain.
  2. While pasta is cooking, heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil and 1 tablespoon of butter in a skillet. Add sliced mushrooms and saute for about 4 minutes or so. Mushrooms will be browned on both sides. Salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Add in the remaining butter and olive oil to the mushrooms in the pan.
  4. Reduce the heat to low, and stir in the shredded parmesan cheese until itâ€™s melted.
  5. Stir in cooked pasta.
  6. Drizzle with a little olive oil. Serve hot, garnished with fresh chives, parsley and more parmesan.
